Phase 1 leverage 2 module hiện có: (1) FnB Menu làm catalog source cho spa product (extend Spa Category + repurpose Cooking Time → Duration); (2) Time Slot làm scheduling engine (1 therapist = 1 Resource row, reuse Resource CRUD).
